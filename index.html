Sir Time Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989. Sir Tim is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) , A Web standards organization founded in 1994 which develops interoperable technologies ( specifications , goiudlines , software , and tools ) to lead the Wed to its full potential . He is the Director of the World Wide Web Foundation which was launched in 2009 to coordinate efforts to further the potential of the World Wide Web to benefit humanity .

in 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal Society . He has be then recipient of several international awards including the Japan Prize , The Prince of Asturias Foundation Prize , The Millenium Technology Prize and Germany's Die Quadriga Award . In 2004 he was knighted my H.M Queen Elizabeth and in 2007 he was awarded the Order of Merit In 2009 he was elected a forgein associate of the National Academy of Sciences .
